FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What age range of children does the pediatric hospital serve?

Little Wonder Kids Pediatric Hospital provides care for children from birth to adolescence, offering specialized services tailored to meet the unique needs of each age group

What service does the hospital offer?

Little Wonder Kids Pediatric Hospital offers a comprehensive range of services, including pediatric primary care, specialty care, neonatal intensive care, pediatric surgery, mergency care, behaviour health services, rehabilitation services and more

How do I schedule an appointment for my child?

To schedule an appointment for your child, you can contact our appointment scheduling department by phone or through our online patient portal. Our friendly staff willassist you in dinding a convenient appointment time with one of our pediatric providers

When visiting Little Wonder Kids Pediatric Hospital for an appointment, please remember to bring your child's insurance information, immunization records, any relevant medical records or test results, a list of current medications, and any questions or concerns you may have for the healthcare provider

What measures are in place to ensure the safety and well=being of patients during their visit?

Little Wonder Kids Pediatric Hospital follows strict infection control protocols and safety measures to protect the health and safety measures to protect the health and safety of our patients, families and staff. This includes enhanced cleaning and disinfection procedures, mandatory mask-wearing, social distancing measures and screening protocols for COVID-19 symptoms

What support services are available for families during their child's hospital stay?

Little Wonder Kids Pediatric Hospital offers a variety of support services for families, including child life services, social work support, counseling services, family resource centers and support groups. These services are designed to help families cope with hospitalization, navigate the healthcare system and access resources and support

How do I get involved and support the pediatric hospitals mission?

There are many ways to get involved and support our hospital's mission, including making a donation, volunteering your time and talents, participating in fundraising evens and advocating for children's healthcare needs in our community. Your support helps us continue to provide high-quality care to children and families in need
